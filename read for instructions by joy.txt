Connections:
DHT11 data pin to D2 (GPIO 4 on the ESP8266)
Soil moisture sensor analog output to A0 (Analog input on ESP8266)
Motor control pin to D6 (GPIO 12) via a relay
LED pin to D5 (GPIO 14)
Power the DHT11 and soil sensor using the 3.3V and GND pins of the ESP8266.

Change SSID and Password as per your wifi credentials.
